(1.6)
Show that if $Z_0[J]$ is given by
\begin{equation*}
Z_0[J]=\exp\left(
-\frac{1}{2}\int d^4x\,d^4y\,J(x)\Delta(x-y)J(y)\right)
\tag{1.49}
\end{equation*}
where $\Delta(x)=\Delta(-x)$ then
\begin{equation*}
\frac{\delta Z_0[J]}{\delta J(z_1)}
=-\left[
\int d^4y\,\Delta(z_1-y)J(y)
\right]Z_0[J]
\tag{1.50}
\end{equation*}
